Paradise On Earth

Loacated on the eastern coastal border of Africa, Wakanda is a full of mountains, waterfalls, flora and fauna and beautiful bluish shores.

Wakanda Resort is nested among the hill slopes, with views to the great shores and cuddling clouds.

Come visit Wakanda and you will be in Paradise on the Earth, enjoying each and every moment.

About Wakanda Resort

Wakanda resort has 20 years of history. Started in the year 1999 by the great T.Challa, our resort offer great entertainment and great food.

We have beautiful spa with wonderful therapists, dance studio and yoga studio overlooking the beach, well equipped outdoor gym with skilled personal trainors.

We made peace with Nature to make our resort a magical place on Earth for you.

Come dive in the serene beaches, enjoy the food and activities.
