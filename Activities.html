The Iguaza waterfalls extend for almost 3km with beautiful flora and fauna. The noise of the water will not quickly leave your memory, along with the unique rainforest flora and fauna. Trekking , hiking along the mountains are some of the activities you can enjoy with your family.

Zambia is just one of the African landscapes, most beautiful in Wakanda.It’s landlocked but three great rivers flow through – the Zambezi, Kafue and Luangwa.It’s home to a number of national parks where you can get up close with both predators and prey.

Uganda might be small place in Wakanda, but it has a lot to offer to tourists.Here you’ll find the source of the Nile, Lake Victoria which is Africa's largest lake and the continent’s tallest range of mountains, the Rwenzoris.

our resort located in driving distance to the most beautiful beach in africa the Nungwi Beach. The sunsets are beautiful and be ready to admire. There are a lot of beach activities to do on beaches. Para sailing, water surfing, water sports, water skiing , jet skiing etc...are some of them you can do.
